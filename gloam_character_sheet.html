                  Rules Quick-Reference

                  Test Resolution

                  • Success: Total 14 or higher.
                  • Great Success: First draw 14+ AND matching Suit.
                  • Push: Draw 2nd card if total < 14.
                  • Great Failure: Fail after Pushing. (+1 Resolve)

                  Difficulty (Penalty)

                  • Standard: 0
                  • Tough: -2 | Hard: -4
                  • Extreme: -6
                  • Opposed: -[Opponent Stat]

                  XP Advancement

                  • Vocation Talent: 5 XP
                  • Other Talent: 10 XP
                  • Earn XP: Participate, Goal, Survival.

                  Combat Actions

                  • Move: Discard a card to move squares = Speed.
                  • Attack: Swords Test vs. Initiative. (Great Success = +1 Wound)
                  • Cast Spell: Spend Resolve. Opposed Wands Test if on enemy.
                  • Grapple/Shove: Opposed Swords Test to immobilize or push.

                  Responses & Special

                  • Dodge: Opposed Coins Test to evade harm.
                  • Riposte: If missed, play a card to immediately Opposed Attack.
                  • Called Shot: Opposed Swords Test to aim for specific unarmored body part.
